Hi, I'm Clark Caughey and I am a User Interaction Designer based in Belfast, Northern Ireland.

I studied at the University of Ulster and since I have been designing and building great websites and applications at Reflex Studios, Rumble Labs and Sugar Rush Creative.

My main hobby used to be ice hockey however since hanging up the skates I have been pressuring my interests in photography and custom cars. I have built a number of modified Volkswagens and I am the founder of automotive media blog Fitted State.

I write my code in HTML and CSS, design interfaces with Sketch and have experience in developing in both PHP and Ruby based environments.

Some of the brands I have worked with in the past include BBC, Belfast Giants, Lonely Planet & Radio Times although I love to work with start ups with fresh ideas.

I'm a strong believer in a less is more approach and I always aim to lead the user on a journey, allowing them (and you) to achieve goals in a clear and desirable manner. This is achieved with an agile development plan, detailed userflow processes, interaction sketches and finally a working prototype.

I am currently available for freelance work. If you have a potential project that you'd like to talk to me about then please get in touch.
